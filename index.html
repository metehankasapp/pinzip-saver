How it works

Open a Pinterest board, profile, search page, feed, or pin page.

Click Select to show image checkboxes directly on the page.

Pick pins manually or use Select visible and Collect 200.

Click Download ZIP to save the selected Pinterest images.

What is PinZip Saver?

PinZip Saver is a Pinterest image downloader Chrome extension. It is designed for people who need to save Pinterest images for mood boards, design references, visual research, personal archives, and inspiration collections. It downloads images you can already access in your browser and does not bypass private accounts, paywalls, or Pinterest access controls.
